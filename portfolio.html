    Tic-Tac-Toe

    Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two - player strategy
    game developed using HTML ,CSS and
    JavaScript. The game consist of a 3 X 3 grid
    where players take turns marking cells with an
    'X' or 'O'. The first player to align their three
    marks vertically , horizontally or diagonally
    wins. The game also features a reset button,
    responsive layout , and visual highlights for
    winning combinations, making it simple and
    engaging.
